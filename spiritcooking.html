Send Your Children for Our Spirit Cooking Dinner Sessions

The Spirit Cooking Dinner is a weekly dinner held at the Community Centre basement floor. There, we serve various foods, such as goat soup, roast goat and potato salad. We cordially invite your children to attend this dinner, as it allows them to bond with our staff, allowing them to learn many things that school will not teach them. We hope that you and your children will benefit from our Spirit Cooking Dinner sessions.
